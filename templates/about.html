About AI STEGANOGRAPHY

Next-Generation Multi-Modal Steganography

The Project

AI STEGANOGRAPHY is an advanced research project focused on Multi-Modal Steganography. Unlike traditional Least Significant Bit (LSB) methods which are easily detectable by statistical analysis, our platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to embed data within cover media in a way that mimics natural noise and textures.

AI-Driven Security

We use deep learning models (CNNs and GANs) to identify the most redundant areas in an image or 3D model. By embedding secrets in these complex regions, we ensure that the visual impact is virtually zero.

Neural Embedding

Our neural networks learn the statistical profile of the cover media. This allows the secret data to blend seamlessly into the pixel/vertex structure, making it resistant to modern steganalysis tools.
